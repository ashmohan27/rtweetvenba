III. Create Pie Chart and Bar Plot of Most-Used Twitter Platforms¶

In order to create the charts, a data frame of only the device platforms and the frequency of their use needs to be created, with another column added calculating the proportion.¶

Use .value_counts() function. Use .reset_index() to ensure that the first column is 'Platform' and the second 'Freq.' Below, the first five rows (top 5 platforms used) are printed. (Source: DataCamp)¶
In [13]:
platfreq = df2['Platform'].value_counts()

dfplatfreq = platfreq.to_frame().reset_index()

dfplatfreq = dfplatfreq.rename(columns= {'index': 'Platform','Platform':'Freq'})

dfplatfreq=dfplatfreq.assign(Prop=dfplatfreq.Freq*100.00/dfplatfreq.Freq.sum())
